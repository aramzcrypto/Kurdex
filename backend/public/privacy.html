# Privacy Policy for Kurdex Markets **Effective Date: March 29, 2026** Kurdex Markets ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our mobile application. ## 1. Information We Collect The App **does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII)**. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or phone number. ### AdMob Data The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect certain data for advertising purposes, including your Device ID, location (general), and interaction with ads. For more information, please visit [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy). ## 2. Push Notifications If you choose to enable push notifications for price alerts, we store an anonymous "Push Token" generated by your device on our secure servers. This token cannot be used to identify you personally and is only used to deliver alerts you have specifically requested. ## 3. Third-Party Services The App pulls market data from various third-party sources (e.g., Yahoo Finance, CoinGecko). No personal information is shared with these providers. ## 4. Security We use industry-standard security measures to protect the anonymous data (like alert settings) stored on our servers. ## 5. Contact Us If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: **support@kurdex.app**
